Advantages of V-model:

1. Easy and simple to use.
2. Saves alot of time when it comes to testing designing and planning.
3. Avoids the downward flow of the defects.
4. Works well for small projects.
5. Has proactive defect tracking.

Disadvantages of V-model:

1. Not very flexible.
2. No early prototypes of a software can be produced.
3. If any changes happen in midway, then the test documents along with requirement documents has to be updated.
